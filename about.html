WHO WE ARE?

Infinium Associates is an Human Resource and Manpower Consulting firm rendering quality recruitments solutions to organisations globally. We support our client’s vision by creating innovative and flexible human resource solutions. These solutions support business growth and commercial targets, whilst balancing the company’s interest and line department requirements.

The company has built a stellar reputation for its high ethical standards, specialized focus and unparalleled service. Emerged As one of the truly global specialized staffing firms focusing exclusively in professional and blue-collared positions for our clients spread globally with a sizable presence across different industry levels with footprints in competitive markets of India, Middle East and the African region .

Managed by a team of qualified specialist consultants with several years of training and experience both in India and abroad. Through a unique combination of skills, competencies, knowledge and favourable experience, Infinium is able to offer exceptional recruitment solutions.

The company has its presence in Mumbai,New Delhi,Riyadh and Dubai with a strong network of associate offices covering locations like India,Nepal,Philippine and Pakistan .
