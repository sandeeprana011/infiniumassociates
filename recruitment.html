Recruitment Procedure

  1. Home
  2. Recruitment Procedure

Reference Checks

We conduct at least two to three reference interviews with peers, managers and direct reports to generate a comprehensive profile of the final candidate. The reference report highlights the person's strengths and weaknesses, which in turn helps the client communicate effectively and prepare the candidate for success.

Search and Research Strategy

Using our database and through other sources of information, contact is made with the prospective candidates to determine the suitability. We then conduct an extensive interview to evaluate their qualifications and level of interest in the client's position. We then shortlist the candidates and prepare reports on them and the market as a whole.

Orientation Session

Every selected candidate sourced by us goes through an extensive orientation session about the culture,laws and ethos as per the country of his deployment. e.g. Saudi Arabia being the abode of Islamic faith, all non-Muslim candidates are meticulously acquainted with the principal faith, the local laws, the customs and the proper code of conduct, strict adherence to these is forcefully stressed.

Need Assessment

The consultation with the client includes spending time with the managers and function team(s) to thoroughly understand the client's business model, marketing strategy, financial strategy, reporting procedures, position specification and candidate qualifications.

Compensation Negotiation

We provide guidance on compensation, taking into account the budget requirements and market rates in the strictest of confidence. We will even conduct compensation negotiations with the final candidate including managing any unrealistic expectations.

Candidate Presentation

We present the shortlisted candidates, along with detailed profiles of their background, work history and assessment of their qualifications.
